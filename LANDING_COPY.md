## FAQ

### Do I need to create an account?

No. The audit works before login or email capture.

### Why do you ask for email after the result?

The tool should prove value first. Email is only needed to save, send, or follow up on the report.

### Are the recommendations generated by AI?

The math is rule-based. AI is only used to summarize the result in plain English.

### Will this expose private company data?

Public audit links strip identifying details like email and company name.

### What if I am already spending efficiently?

The report will say that. The tool should not manufacture fake savings.
